  • +shippingInfoForChargeWithAddress:shippingMethod:

    When creating a charge on your backend, you can attach shipping information to prevent fraud on a physical good. You can use this method to turn your user’s shipping address and selected shipping method into a hash suitable for attaching to a charge. You should pass this to your backend, and use it as the shipping parameter when creating a charge. - see: https://stripe.com/docs/api#create_charge-shipping
